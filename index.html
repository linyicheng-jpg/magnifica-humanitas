Magnifica Humanitas · 2026

人类，站在岔路口Humanity at the Crossroads

  • 技术正在加速。人的方向感却没有同步更新。Technology is accelerating. Our moral compass is not.
  • 真正的问题不是：机器能做什么？The real question is not what machines can do.
  • 而是：我们要成为什么样的人？It is who we choose to become.

01 · The New Babel

当能力成为新的偶像When Power Becomes the New Idol

  • 技术开始分配机会、注意力与话语权。Technology now allocates opportunity, attention and voice.
  • 少数人控制基础设施。多数人承担后果。A few control the infrastructure. Many bear the consequences.
  • 效率一旦失去尺度，就会变成支配。Efficiency without measure becomes domination.

02 · Two Cities

巴别塔，还是耶路撒冷？Babel or Jerusalem?

统一 · 封闭 · 攀升 Uniform · Closed · Rising upward

文明的高度，不由塔尖决定。
A civilization is not measured by its tallest tower.

协作 · 多元 · 修复 Plural · Collaborative · Rebuilt together

而由谁被允许留在城中决定。
It is measured by who is welcomed inside.

03 · Irreducible Dignity

人，无法被压缩成变量A Person Cannot Be Reduced to Variables
  • 人的价值，不来自效率。Human worth does not come from efficiency.
  • 尊严先于计算。Dignity comes before computation.

04 · Accountability

没有透明，就没有责任No Transparency, No Accountability

  • 决策可以自动化。责任不能。Decisions may be automated. Responsibility cannot.
  • 黑箱不是技术细节，而是公共问题。Opacity is not a technical detail. It is a public issue.
  • 人必须保留理解、质疑与拒绝的权利。People must retain the right to understand, question and refuse.

05 · Truth

没有真实，就没有共同体Without Truth, There Is No Community

  • 信息更多，不等于我们更接近真相。More information does not mean more truth.
  • 当事实被稀释，信任首先崩塌。When facts dissolve, trust collapses first.
  • 验证、倾听、对话，是新的公民能力。Verification, listening and dialogue are civic skills.

06 · Work

让机器辅助劳动，而不是抹除劳动者Let Machines Assist Work, Not Erase Workers

  • 工作不只是收入。它也是参与、成长与尊严。Work is more than income. It is participation, growth and dignity.
  • 自动化应该减少负担。Automation should reduce burdens.
  • 而不是把人变成成本。It should not reduce people to costs.

07 · Freedom

自由，先从守住注意力开始Freedom Begins with Attention

  • 最深的控制，不一定来自命令。The deepest control does not always come from commands.
  • 当注意力被商品化，内心会逐渐失去安静。When attention is commodified, inner silence fades.

08 · War

责任不能藏在瞄准线之后Responsibility Cannot Hide Behind a Targeting Line

  • 当发动攻击变得更容易，克制就变得更稀缺。When force becomes easier, restraint becomes rarer.
  • 最终决定必须属于人。The final decision must remain human.

09 · Encounter

连接，不等于共同体Connection Is Not Community

  • 网络制造接近。Networks create proximity.
  • 共同体需要承诺。Community requires commitment.
  • 被动的相互依赖，必须转化为主动的团结。Passive interdependence must become chosen solidarity.

11 · Hope from Below

真正的进步，不会把任何人留在阴影里True Progress Leaves No One in the Shadows
  • 从最弱小的人开始判断技术。Judge technology from the perspective of the most vulnerable.
  • 人类不是系统的附件。人本身，就是目的。Humanity is not an accessory to the system. The human person is the purpose.
